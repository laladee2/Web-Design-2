HISTORY

MapleStory was developed and released in South Korea and was localized to ten different regions as of 2010.

As other publishers license the game for their region, proxy blocks are put in place (excluding the Korean version, it requires a KSSN), forcing players to play their localized version.On July 7, 2011, LevelUp! Games announced that they will not be renewing the contract for localization in Brazil.


Notable updates

An update entitled Big Bang was released in Korean MapleStory in July 2010 with other versions following suit.[citation needed] Big Bang changed the game's core coding, and changed many aspects of the game.An update entitled Chaos features the introduction of professions, and PvP.
